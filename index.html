^ We all started here. Learn where I started!

I'm an aspiring software engineer & game developer studying Software Engineering & Game Design @ McMaster University. I'm currently growing my domain in graphics / gaming development.

Rollback - It all began with my 4 year old self - doodling characters from my favourite anime shows (NARUTO - "Believe it!") & throwing fits over why I couldn't beat that lvl 3 Jigglypuff in Smash Bros Melee. I've also always been a huge fan of math. I particularly enjoy pattern-based problems!

Carrying forward, this has translated into my passion for software & design - with the hope of breaking into the game industry! (It's a longer story than that - better saved for another time).

My career goal is ultimately to find something that will cultivate my nature to create, to design, to innovate & to problem solve.

My mantra: "It always seems impossible until it's done." - Nelson Mandela
